### 7. Footer - Compliance Statements

#### Company Description
**Before:** "At our pharmaacy, we are dedicated to providing top-notch healthcare solutions with a focus on quality, safety, and personalized care."

**After:** "Mythic Rx is a 503A licensed compounding pharmacy serving healthcare providers with precision sterile and non-sterile formulations. USP 797 & 795 compliant."

#### Added Compliance Section (NEW)
A prominent compliance notice box was added with:

1. **Prescription Acknowledgment Statement:**
"By submitting a prescription or order request to Mythic Pharmacy, you acknowledge that all compounded medications are prepared pursuant to a valid patient-specific prescription, are not FDA-approved, and must be dispensed and used under the supervision of a licensed prescriber. Mythic Pharmacy does not provide medical diagnosis, treatment decisions, or telehealth services."

2. **Licensing Statement:**
"Mythic Pharmacy can only dispense medications in states where we hold an active pharmacy license. Currently licensed in: **Utah, Idaho, Arizona, Colorado, Wyoming, and Hawaii**. Additional states will be added as licenses are approved."

**Key Improvements:**
- Added legally required compliance statements
- Clearly communicated licensing limitations
- Emphasized patient-specific prescription requirement
- Clarified that products are not FDA-approved (as required for compounded medications)

## Compliance Adherence

### Avoided Language
✅ No use of "miracle," "cure," "guaranteed," or "FDA-approved" (for compounded products)
✅ No weight loss outcome promises
✅ No anti-aging claims
✅ No comparative statements suggesting superiority to FDA-approved drugs
✅ No direct-to-consumer marketing language

### Required Elements Included
✅ 503A licensing information
✅ USP 797 & 795 compliance mention
✅ Patient-specific prescription requirement
✅ State licensing disclosure
✅ Non-FDA-approved disclaimer
✅ Prescriber supervision requirement
